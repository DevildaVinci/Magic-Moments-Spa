About Our Sanctuary

Welcome to Magic Moment Spa, where tranquility meets expertise. Nestled in Calangunte, our spa is a haven designed for your ultimate escape. We believe in holistic well-being, offering a curated selection of treatments that harmonize your mind, body, and spirit.

Our highly skilled therapists are dedicated to providing personalized experiences, using premium products and time-honored techniques. Our Spa Services

BALINESE MASSAGE

Balinese massage is a full-body, deep-tissue, holistic massage.

DEEP TISSUE MASSAGE

Deep tissue massage centers on realigning the deeper layers of connective and muscle tissue. It is particularly helpful to continually right and contracted areas such as stiff necks, low back tightness, and sore shoulders.

Aromatherapy Massage

Aromatherapy massage combines two therapeutic practices to create wonderful results. Massage uses pressure and touch to offer healing and stress relief by stimulating the lymphatic, circulatory, nervous, and musculoskeletal systems.

SWEDISH MASSAGE

SWEDISH MASSAGE THERAPY Is The Most Well-Known And Widely Practiced Type Of Therapies. Good And For Good Reason, Swedish Massage Involves Long, Kneading Strokes Combined With Rhythmic Tapping Strokes And Movement Of The Joints.

Thai Massage

Combines broad and targeted acupressure, stimulation and manipulation of energy lines, and assisted yoga postures.

Ayurvedic Oil Massage

Ayurvedic massages are done with medicated herbal oils that have various health benefits. Besides rubbing the body down from head to toe with an herb-infused oil specifically chosen to help balance the doshas, or energies, in your body.

4 Hand Massage

Two therapists will work on you synchronously during a four hand massage session. This ultimately leads to a higher level of relaxation and better muscle tension relief.
